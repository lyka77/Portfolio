Cedarworks Responsive ISO App


The goal of this project was to update the existing ISO app, a platform that the employees used to keep track of customer complaints. My first step was to interview the current users of the app, the employees at Cedarworks, to find out which features work well for them and which are outdated or unused, as well as which features they would like to see added. I took this information and created several prototypes, both low and high-fidelity. I worked to implement the aformentioned changes as well as integrating bootstrap to make the app responsive and mobile-friendly. My work was mostly focused on the look-and feel of the app rather than the actual function, so the functions of my prototype were fairly limited.
