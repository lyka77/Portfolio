Senior CS Major Poster

In my senior year, all CS majors were required to research and present a poster on a topic of our choice relating to computer science. I chose to look into the use of machine learning in the field of brain tumor diagnosis. I was interested in this topic from both a personal standpoint and an academic one. Throughout this project, I learned about the drawbacks of the current diagnostic method, as well as how ML solutions could become more effective methods of diagnosis. This was an individual poster that was submitted to the CS department for reviews. You can read more about the topic and my conclusions below.
